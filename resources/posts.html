PuTTYCyg: An awesome terminal for Windows

2010-10-11

Subtitle

In the past, I’ve used rxvt for my Windows terminal emulator. No more. I’ve discovered PuTTYCyg, a program to let you use PuTTY as a local terminal emulator, integrated with Cygwin. I’ve switched over to it, and I love it! PuTTY is an amazing terminal emulator that’s very full featured, so why only use it for SSH? Why PuTTY doesn’t support this kind of thing natively, I’ll never know.
